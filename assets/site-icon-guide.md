## Recommended Approach

### Option 1: Use Power Platform Official Logo (Best)
Download the official Microsoft Power Platform logo:
- Go to: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=35825
- Or use Microsoft Brand Portal: https://www.microsoft.com/brand/

### Option 2: Custom Icon with Power Apps Theme
Use a design tool to create:
- **Colors**: Purple gradient (#667eea to #764ba2) matching your dark theme
- **Icon**: Lightning bolt or puzzle piece symbolizing Power Platform
- **Size**: 192x192px (minimum), 512x512px (recommended)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
